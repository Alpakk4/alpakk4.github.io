## Privacy Policy

### Last Updated: March 2026**

This policy outlines our commitment to participant privacy and data minimisation.

* **No PII Storage:** No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers is collected or stored on this applications servers. Data is linked only to a users unique deviceID.

* **Ephemeral Location Processing:** Raw GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) are processed ephemerally via serverless functions to identify activity categories.
**Raw coordinates are never stored in a database.**
* **Home Privacy:** The user's "Home" location is stored strictly on the device. The researcher only receives relative distance and bearing data, ensuring the actual start and end points remain unknown and unstored.
* **Data Storage:** Processed activity logs are securely transmitted to Supabase via encrypted channels.
* **Retention & Deletion:** Users may request data deletion until **1st June 2026**. After this date, data is fully anonymised for the final report, and individual records can no longer be identified or,for that reason, deleted.
