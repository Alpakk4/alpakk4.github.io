## Troubleshooting comming issues

You will need a valid deviceID, provided only through being enrolled in the study, to be able to use all the app features.

- **Empty Diary:** The Diary Builder processes data from the previous 24 hours. The app must be running in the background to be able to build a picture of your day. Location permissions must be enabled. Your deviceID must exactly match the one your were provided with at enrollement.

- **Current Location not Displayed\:** Ensure "Background Location" and "Physical Activity" are set to "Always Allow" ,or equivalent, for best performance. Toggle location sharing on/off in the app to force a location update. Location should update with significant change of location.

- **Locked Entries:** Once a diary is submitted, it is locked for data integrity and cannot be amended. You can ammend diary entries as much as you like **before** you submit them.
