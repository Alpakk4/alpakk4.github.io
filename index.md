## Welcome to Pinglo

Welcome to the page for the PingLo research project. This app is designed to explore human activity patterns through journey and visit analysis.

### Participation

This app is intended for use by authorised participants of the MSc study. To access all features, you must enter the unique sequential Device ID provided during your enrollment. To find out more about the study please contact the app developer. [Contact the Developer](/support.html)

### Key Features

- **Automated Diary Synthesis:** Categorises and clusters information from your phone into daily vists with activities, and transit modes.
- **Privacy-First:** Processes location ephemerally to protect your identity.
- **Motion Detection:** Identifies Still, Walking, Cycling, and Automotive travel.
- **Manual Diary Validation** Opportunity for you to confirm/reject the types of locations and activities you performed; and provide additional context to diary entries.
